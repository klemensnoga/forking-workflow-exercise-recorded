# Brownie recipe



## Preparation

- Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C)
- Grease and line an 8×8-inch (20×20 cm) baking pan with parchment paper
- Melt butter and set aside to cool slightly



## Ingredients

- 0.5 cup (115 g) unsalted butter, melted
- 1 cup (200 g) granulated sugar
- 2 large eggs
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 0.33 cup (40 g) unsweetened cocoa powder
- 0.5 cup (65 g) all-purpose flour
- 0.25 tsp salt
- 0.25 tsp baking powder
- 0.5 cup (90 g) chocolate chips or chopped chocolate (optional)



## Instructions

- In a mixing bowl, whisk melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla until smooth
- Add cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder; stir until combined (don’t overmix)
- Fold in chocolate chips if using
- Pour batter into prepared baking pan and spread evenly



## Baking

- Bake for 20–25 minutes at 350°F (175°C)
- Edges should be set, and center slightly soft
- Cool completely in pan before cutting into squares
