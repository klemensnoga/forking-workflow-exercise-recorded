# Apple pie recipe


## Preparation

- Peel and core apples
- Slice apples into 1/4-inch thick slices
- Use about 6-7 medium apples per pie (6-8 cups sliced)
- Toss apple slices with lemon juice to prevent browning


## Ingredients

- Pie shell (double crust or 2 pie crusts)
- 6-7 medium apples (Granny Smith or mix of tart and sweet)
- 0.75 cup sugar
- 0.25 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 0.25 tsp nutmeg
- 0.25 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp butter, cubed
- 1 tbsp lemon juice


## Instructions

- Mix sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt in a bowl
- Add apple slices and lemon juice, toss to coat
- Pour apple mixture into bottom pie crust
- Dot with butter cubes
- Place second pie crust over filling, crimp edges to seal
- Cut slits in top crust for venting


## Baking

- 15 min at 425 F
- 45-50 min at 350 F
- Cool on wire rack before serving
